Copado FAQ

Are you a Copado implementation partner?

We are a Copado Technical Partner (the ISV tier that builds on the platform and publishes to the DevOps Exchange). Copado implementation is something we deliver as a service, not a partner-status claim.

Can you take over an existing Copado pipeline?

Yes. We pick up existing pipelines for release engineering, deployment troubleshooting and managed DevOps — as well as setting Copado up from scratch.

Do you offer Copado training?

Yes — instructor-led team training and certification prep (Copado Fundamentals, Developer, Consultant), remote or on-site in Spain and the EU, built around real release-pipeline practice rather than slideware.
