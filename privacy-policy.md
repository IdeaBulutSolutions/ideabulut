This policy explains what personal data Idea Bulut Solutions collects through this
website, why, and what your rights are. It is provided in good faith for transparency
and is not legal advice.

## 1. Who is the data controller
The data controller is **{{ site.company.legal_name }}** ("Idea Bulut Solutions", "we",
"us"), with its registered office in {{ site.company.location }}.
You can reach us about privacy at **[{{ site.email }}](mailto:{{ site.email }})**.

## 2. What we collect
We only collect what you give us:

- **Contact form:** your name, work email, company (optional), the service you're asking
  about (optional), your message, and your **consent** choice and the time it was given.
- **Booking a call:** when you book a Discovery Chat or a paid consultation, the booking is
  handled by Google Calendar Appointments, which collects the details you enter (such as name
  and email) to schedule the meeting and generate a Google Meet link.

We do **not** run advertising trackers, and we don't sell or share your data for marketing.

## 3. Why we use it, and the legal basis
We use your data **solely to respond to your enquiry** and arrange any call you request.

- **Contact form:** legal basis is your **consent** (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)), which you give via
  the form's consent checkbox, and our **legitimate interest** in responding to business
  enquiries (Art. 6(1)(f)).
- **Bookings:** legal basis is taking steps at your request prior to / during an engagement
  (Art. 6(1)(b)) and consent.

## 4. Where it's stored and who processes it
Your data is stored in **Google Workspace** (Google Sheets, Google Calendar). We use a small
number of processors:

- **Google (Google Workspace, Calendar, Meet)** — form storage and booking.
- **Cloudflare Turnstile** — bot protection on the contact form (no tracking cookies).
- **Stripe** — payment processing for paid consultation bookings (handled through Google
  Calendar Appointments).
- **GitHub Pages** — serves this website.
- **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** — web analytics to understand how the site is used. GA4 is
  loaded **only after you accept analytics cookies** in our cookie banner; if you decline, it
  is not loaded and no analytics cookies are set.

Some of these providers may process data outside the EU/EEA under appropriate safeguards
(e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

## 5. How long we keep it
We keep enquiry data for up to **24 months** after our last contact with you, then delete it —
unless we are required to keep it longer to comply with a legal obligation.

## 6. Your rights
Under the GDPR you have the right to **access, rectify, erase, restrict, object to**, and
**port** your personal data, and to **withdraw consent** at any time. To exercise any of
these, email **[{{ site.email }}](mailto:{{ site.email }})**. You also have the right to
lodge a complaint with the Spanish supervisory authority, the **Agencia Española de
Protección de Datos (AEPD)**.

## 7. Cookies
This site sets **no advertising cookies**. Cloudflare Turnstile may set a strictly-necessary
token to verify you're not a bot. We use **Google Analytics 4**, which sets analytics cookies —
these load **only if you accept** them in the cookie banner shown on your first visit. If you
decline, no analytics cookies are set. You can change your choice at any time by clearing this
site's cookies in your browser.

## 8. Changes
We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.
